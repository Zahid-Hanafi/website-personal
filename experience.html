Volunteer Outreach Programs

I also participated in outreach programs as a volunteer within the Public Health Unit by Hospital Canselor Tuanku Muhriz. These programs, part of the Inisiatif Komuniti Malaysia Madani Sihat (IKMAS), took place in three locations:

  • Kampung Orang Asli Pos Tenau, Perak: Conducted health screenings & awareness campaigns.

  • Felda Batang Padang Besout, Perak: Engaged in community health education sessions.

  • Felda Sungai Behrang, Perak: Assisted in public health surveys & health checkups.
