About Me

History

My name is Muhammad Zahid Hanafi, and I was born in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, on 03/04/2004. Growing up in a family that valued education and creativity, I learned the importance of teamwork and resilience, being one of 3 siblings. My childhood was filled with adventures exploring urban parks and city landmarks, which sparked my fascination with technology and how cities evolve.

From a young age, I dreamed of becoming a Lecturer, which led me to pursue a passion for teaching and continue my studies with a diploma in Information Management. A key turning point came in 2021 when the SPM opened my eyes to the significance of being smart in order to teach others. Encouraged by my family and mentors, I deepened my passion for education and helping others understand complex concepts.

My passion for education grew throughout school, inspiring me to pursue it professionally. Although I am still a student, I am deeply committed to my studies, with the goal of becoming a lecturer at a university where I can share my knowledge and help shape the minds of future generations.

Today, my focus is on education, with the goal of contributing to the academic field and leaving a positive impact on the world by shaping the minds of future generations.
